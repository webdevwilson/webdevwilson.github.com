--- layout: post title: No Time to Test? published: false meta: _edit_last: "1" _edit_lock: "1299793283" tags: - News type: post status: draft --- If I were to guess (and I will), if you asked developers who do not write tests, why they are not writing them. 90% of them would say they do not have enough time. Most people feel like they hardly have time to write the features, much less the code to properly test them. The idea that testing takes a huge amount of time is probably one of the most pervasive myths in our industry.
